Privacy Policy – FASTER Cycling

Last updated: 27 February 2026

FASTER Cycling (“FASTER”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

By using the FASTER app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

⸻

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:
	•	Email address
	•	User ID
	•	Authentication information

Training Data

To generate adaptive workouts, we process:
	•	Ride history
	•	Training metrics (e.g., power, duration, intensity)
	•	Goal information
	•	Workout completion data

If you connect third-party services (e.g., Strava), we access training-related data that you authorize.

Technical Information

We may collect:
	•	Device type
	•	Operating system version
	•	App version
	•	Crash logs
	•	Diagnostic data

This information is used to maintain performance and stability.

⸻

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:
	•	Generate personalized cycling workouts
	•	Adapt your progression toward your goal
	•	Improve app performance and reliability
	•	Respond to support requests
	•	Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data.

⸻

3. Third-Party Services

FASTER may use third-party services such as:
	•	Authentication providers
	•	Cloud storage providers
	•	Analytics and crash reporting services
	•	Subscription and payment providers (Apple)

These services process data according to their own privacy policies.

If you connect Strava or other external platforms, your data is processed according to the permissions you grant.

⸻

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data.

We may share limited data with trusted service providers strictly for the purpose of operating the app (e.g., hosting, analytics, infrastructure).

⸻

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to:
	•	Provide the service
	•	Maintain your account
	•	Comply with legal obligations

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.
